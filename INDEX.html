About Us


PSG Institute of management started out as a department of management under PSG College of Technology in the year 1965. We are one of the oldest B-schools in India, producing top-notch management graduates. With a vast widespread distinguished alumni across the globe, we strive to be a visionary among the elite private B-schools in India.






About ALPS(Active Learning Program)



Take some time off the hustle and bustle of city life, and revitalize yourself at the foothills of the western ghats in the lands of Anaikatti! Your academic and non-academic skills will be put to test, with a series of exciting activities, designed to gauge creativity, endurance, and most importantly, teamwork. Come and make your presence felt by emerging victorious amidst the stirring competition.
